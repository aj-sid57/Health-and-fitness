STRENGTH & CONDITIONING. fitness_center

Strength training makes your muscles work harder than they usually do. You also build endurance so your muscles can work hard without making you tired afterward.

Moving through body conditioning exercises trains your body to open up and move in different ways.

benifits of strength &conditioning:
Makes you stronger
Burns calories efficiently
Decreases abdominal fat
Can help you appear leaner
Decreases your risk of falls
Lowers your risk of injury
Helps manage your blood sugar levels

FLEXIBILITY sports_gymnastics

Flexibility training involves activities that aim to stretch your muscles until they're loosened up, and your body becomes lithe and limber.

goal of flexibility training is to achieve strength combined with flexibility that can allow the client to better control his or her movements

benefits of flexibility training:
Less pain
Improved posture and balance
A positive state of mind
Improved physical performance

virat kohli

Kohli follows a 5-6 day workout week with one day for rest. To maintain that sculpted physique, Virat includes a mix of cardio, weight and strength training in his workout sessions.

taking about his fitness kohli said "From 2015 I changed my training again. I started lifting, snatching, cleaning and dead lifting. It was unbelievable. I saw the result. I remember running after a ball in a Test series in Sri Lanka and I felt more power in my legs. It was, like, 'wow'. This training is addictive. The last year-and-a-half it has taken my game to another level," he explained.
